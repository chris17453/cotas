## About

Computer Accounting Solutions (CAS) is another purveyor of Advanced Accounting software, publishing **Advanced Accounting 7** — a GUI/Windows version of the Advanced Accounting product line built on TAS Professional 7.

## Advanced Accounting 7

- Complete double-entry, accrual-based accounting system
- Part of an integrated family of Computer Accounting Solutions products
- Written using **TAS Professional 7** application development system
- Designed for small business needs
- Affordable, modifiable accounting package

### Key Features

- **Modifiable source code** — With TAS Professional 7 and the Advanced Accounting 7 source code, users can customize screens, menus, and programs
- **Screen/Report Editor** — Change DFM (GUI screens) and RTM (report) forms without programming; modify colors, fonts, field positions, add logos
- **Scalable** — CAS provides custom programming for any needed modifications
- **Integrated product family** — Designed to grow with the user's business

### Products Available
